📹 CAMERA & LIVE STREAMING (Like TikTok)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STARTING A LIVE STREAM:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Go to Live tab (📺)
2. Tap "🎬 GO LIVE" button at the top
3. Camera starts automatically
4. Default: Front camera (selfie mode)

CAMERA CONTROLS:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
🎤 Microphone Toggle
   • Tap to turn microphone ON/OFF
   • Shows as "🎤 Mic ON" or "🔇 Mic OFF"
   
📷 Camera Switch
   • Tap to switch between front and back camera
   • Front (🤳) = Selfie/Face camera
   • Back (📷) = Rear camera
   • Switch instantly while streaming!
   
🔴 GO LIVE / END LIVE
   • Large button in center
   • Tap to START the stream (goes red when live)
   • Tap to END the stream
   
👥 Viewer Count
   • Shows real-time viewer count
   • Updates while you're streaming
   • Displays as "👥 [number]"

FEATURES:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

• Toggle in settings if needed

LOGIN NOT WORKING?

• Try sign up with new account
